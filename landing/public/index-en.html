Live Match

Experience every match in real time. Make up to 5 substitutions, change formation and adjust team mentality during the game. Every tactical decision has consequences on the pitch.

Transfers & Scouting

Send scouts to find players, negotiate transfers with counter-offers, loan players in and out, or sign pre-contracts with free agents. Manage renewals and contracts.

Youth Academy

Receive new prospects every season with hidden stats that are progressively revealed. At the end of each season, decide who gets promoted to the first team, goes on loan, or is released.

7 Competitions

La Liga, Segunda División, Copa del Rey, Supercopa, Champions League, Europa League and Conference League. Promotions, relegations and playoffs.

Club Economy

Allocate your budget across transfers, youth academy, medical staff, scouts and facilities. Each investment tier unlocks real advantages. Your performance determines next season's revenue.

Real 2025/26 Data

Real La Liga and Segunda División squads with over 1,000 players. Skills based on market value and age. Realistic development, potential and decline every season.

The Game in Action

Manage every aspect of your club: tactics, transfers, youth academy, finances and European competitions.
